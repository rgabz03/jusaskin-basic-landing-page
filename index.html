  • App Description

    A platform that gives you the opportunity to learn from the expert(professionals, celebrities, influencers). Allowing you to ask your favorite expert questions you have been dying to ask. The cost per question is set by the expert themselves which provides the expert the opportunity to control the number of questions they receive. Experts then respond in a video up to 60 seconds. The video will be automatically posted on both of your accounts. Every video like costs users 2 cents. This fee is split between you for asking such a wonderful question and the expert for providing such valuable feedback. This encourages you to ask more lovely questions the rest of the world is also dying to know. The better the question the better the response the more you [L]earn.
